Here is a graded list of classical guitar repertoire, from Grade 1 to 10, to help you plan your studies. This list includes well-regarded pieces, their composers, and where you can likely find them, with a focus on materials that might be available as open-source scores.

### Understanding Classical Guitar Grading

Similar to piano, major music examination boards like the Associated Board of the Royal Schools of Music (ABRSM) and the Royal Conservatory of Music (RCM) have their own graded systems for classical guitar. The following list provides a progressive path of study.

### Grade 1

Beginner-level pieces focusing on basic right- and left-hand technique.

- **Ferdinando Carulli:** Andantino, Waltz.
- **Dionisio Aguado:** Waltz.
- **Fernando Sor:** Study No. 1, Op. 60; Study No. 2, Op. 60; Study No. 3, Op. 60.

### Grade 2

Introduction of more complex rhythms and fuller chords.

- **Matteo Carcassi:** Andantino.
- **Mauro Giuliani:** Allegro, Etude No. 1.
- **Ferdinando Carulli:** Petit Caprice.
- **Fernando Sor:** Study No. 8, Op. 60; Study No. 9, Op. 60.

### Grade 3

Developing musical expression with a wider range of notes.

- **Anonymous:** Spanish Romance (a perennial favorite, also known as _Romanza_).
- **Francisco Tárrega:** Study in C.
- **Matteo Carcassi:** Etude in A minor.
- **Dionisio Aguado:** Etude in A minor.

### Grade 4

Intermediate pieces that require more refined technique.

- **Christian Petzold:** Minuet.
- **J.S. Bach (arrangements):** Bouree in E minor (from Lute Suite BWV 996).
- **Gaspar Sanz:** Españoleta.
- **Robert de Visée:** Bourrée.

### Grade 5

More complex musical textures and longer pieces.

- **Robert de Visée:** Prelude.
- **J.S. Bach:** Bouree from BWV 996.
- **Matteo Carcassi:** Etude No. 3, Op. 60; Etude No. 7, Op. 60.
- **Fernando Sor:** Study in D Major, Op. 35, No. 17; Study in B Minor, Op. 35, No. 22.

### Grade 6

Early-advanced repertoire with greater technical and musical demands.

- **Francisco Tárrega:** Adelita.
- **J.S. Bach:** Bourrée from Lute Suite BWV 996.
- **Matteo Carcassi:** Etudes from Op. 60.
- **Agustín Barrios Mangoré:** Preludio in D Minor.

### Grade 7

Advanced pieces requiring a high level of technical skill and interpretation.

- **J.S. Bach:** Prelude from Cello Suite No. 1, BWV 1007.
- **Johann Kaspar Mertz:** An Malvina, from _Bardenklänge_, Op. 13.
- **Heitor Villa-Lobos:** Prélude No. 1.
- **Francisco Tárrega:** Lágrima.
- **Manuel Ponce:** Scherzino Mexicano.

### Grade 8

Pre-conservatory level repertoire.

- **Francisco Tárrega:** Capricho Árabe.
- **J.S. Bach:** Prelude in D minor, BWV 999.
- **Leo Brouwer:** Un Dia de Noviembre.
- **Isaac Albéniz:** Asturias (Leyenda).

### Grade 9

Diploma-level pieces for advanced students.

- **Agustín Barrios Mangoré:** La Catedral (complete).
- **Heitor Villa-Lobos:** Etude No. 1.
- **J.S. Bach:** Fugue from Violin Sonata No. 1, BWV 1001 (arr. for guitar).
- **Leo Brouwer:** Elogio de la Danza.

### Grade 10 and Beyond

Advanced repertoire suitable for undergraduate and graduate study.

- **Joaquín Rodrigo:** Concierto de Aranjuez (for guitar and orchestra).
- **J.S. Bach:** Chaconne in D minor (from Violin Partita No. 2, BWV 1004).
- **Heitor Villa-Lobos:** The complete 12 Etudes.
- **Antonio Lauro:** Venezuelan Waltzes, such as _Natalia/Vals Criollo_.
- **Isaac Albéniz:** Suite Española, Op. 47 (arr. for guitar).
